About This Workshop

The Workshop on Modern Trends in Cryptography will take place on June 13-14, 2019 at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. The Workshop features invited talks by established researchers from around the world, covering various aspects of modern cryptography.

Target audience: Researchers and students interested in cryptography.

Coffee breaks and lunches will be provided.
